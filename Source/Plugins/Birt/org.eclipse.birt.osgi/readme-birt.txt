How to make the birt-api.jar
----------------------------

Combine the following ReportEngine/lib jars:

coreapi.jar
modelapi.jar
dteapi.jar
scriptapi.jar
engineapi.jar

How to make the birt-osgi jar
-----------------------------

Zip up the ReportEngine/plugins and ReportEngine/configuration dir without plugins/org.eclipse.birt.api_1.0.0.jar included.
