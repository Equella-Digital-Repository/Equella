Title: Zou ba! Visiting China: Is this your first visit? 
Description: Watch two friends talking about holidays. Look at countries they have visited such as China, Great Britain, United States of America, France, Germany, Thailand and South Korea. Investigate how to ask and answer questions about countries visited. This learning object is one in a series of five objects. 
Topics: Chinese characters; Chinese languages; Conversations; Countries; Dialogue; Holidays; Questions; Tourism; Travel; Vocabulary 
Keywords: Chinese characters, Chinese languages, Conversations, Countries, Dialogue, Holidays, International travel, Questions, Tourism, Travel, Vocabulary 
Learning area: Languages Other Than English 
Strand: Oral interaction; Reading and responding; Understanding culture; Understanding language 
Concept: Arrangements; Characters; Chinese; Interactions; Language systems; Pinyin; Questions; Sequences; Social situations 
Audience user level: 5; 6; 7; 8; 9; 10  Audience type: Student 
Age range: 9-16  Audience sector: School 
Key learning objectives:
  • Students explore the Chinese names for several countries.
  • Students investigate how to ask and answer questions about whether they have visited a country or not.
Educational value:
  • Introduces the Chinese names for several countries.
  • Provides a series of English questions to test comprehension of a dialogue.
  • Models spoken questions and answers about countries visited.
  • Provides opportunities for students to practise the construction of spoken questions and answers.
  • Includes a listening and reading challenge where students select Chinese characters to match spoken dialogue.
  • Provides audio, Chinese characters, pinyin and language explanations for all target language content.
  • Gives all instructions in English.
